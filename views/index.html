Professional Biography

André L. F. Batista works at Federal Institute of Triangulo Mineiro in Brazil as full professor of Computer Science. His current work has an emphasis on Computer Science Education as well Game-Based Learning. Recent work includes the development of a game-based learning framework to teach programming fundamentals in introductory programming classes.

He is also actively involved in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning and have run a few projects in this area. The most recent one is the design and development of an computer-aided diagnosis software using artificial intelligence in clinical imaging.

A third strand of expertise is in educational robotics, have run a few projects in this field using Arduino and Lego Mindstorms. The most distinguished one is the use of a motion sensor (like MS Kinect) to remotely control robotics structures.

His teaching areas includes Artificial Intelligence; Web Programming I and II; Database I; Programming I and II; Data Structures.
